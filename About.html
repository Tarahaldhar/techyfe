Meet Our Founder — Tara

With over 5-6 years of experience in the IT industry, Mai Tara's journey has been one of constant learning, bold decisions, and unshakable determination. Like many driven individuals, she began her career working in a well-established IT company, gaining valuable technical knowledge and industry insights.

But alongside her job, her entrepreneurial spirit never rested. She spent countless hours freelancing, learning how to manage projects independently, building client relationships, and sharpening her skills beyond the 9 to 5 routine.

Still, the idea of launching something of her own always stayed in the back of her mind. The thought of starting a company came with excitement — but also fear. Fear of the unknown, fear of failure. But as they say, growth begins at the edge of your comfort zone.

Gathering her courage, she made the bold decision to leave her job and follow her dream. What started with hesitation soon turned into a full-fledged journey of building a startup from scratch — driven by passion, expertise, and a belief in creating meaningful digital solutions.

Today, under her leadership, we’re proud to deliver innovative Web Development and AI Solutions that empower businesses to grow smarter and faster. Her story reminds us — it’s not easy to take risks, but with hard work and belief, success follows.
